Member Benefits

Powered by Unlock Protocol

Member Benefits is a free Shopify app that lets merchants easily integrate memberships to their online stores using Unlock Protocol.
Think of memberships as locks, and everyone with a key as a member.
The keys are NFTs (ERC-721).
